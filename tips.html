Health & Fitness Tips 🌿

Stay consistent, stay smart, stay strong.

🥦 Nutrition

Eat whole foods, limit processed items, and hydrate well. Include protein in every meal.

🏃 Exercise

Mix strength training with cardio. Follow a structured weekly plan and track your progress.

💧 Hydration

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Hydration boosts performance and recovery.

🛌 Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Your body grows and repairs while you rest.

⚠️ Avoid Overtraining

Recovery is just as important as workouts. Listen to your body and take rest days seriously.

🧘‍♂️ Mental Health

Practice mindfulness or meditation. A calm mind supports physical health and long-term goals.
